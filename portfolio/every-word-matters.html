every word matters

Overview

Challenge

Set up a blog interviewing Content Designers and UX Writers from across the industry that’s helpful for anyone looking to join, or already in, the industry — every word matters.

Basic process

  • Decide on a name for the blog
  • Look at other interview sites to see what questions they ask people in similar fields
  • Compare hosting and blog platform options
  • Set up a publication on Medium
  • Set up a Twitter account for the blog
  • Design a visual identity
  • Start listing out people you want to interview, along with how to contact them
  • Write out as many questions as I could think of
  • Edit the questions down to a more manageable amount
  • Start contacting people to see if they’d be interested in taking part
  • If they are, send them the questions and request a photo of them
  • Design the header images so the interviewees name, company and photo can be placed in
  • Edit the response from interviewees
  • Draft the response on Medium, writing tags and adding it to the publication
  • Review the interview one last time
  • Publish the interview
  • Share the interview on Twitter

Results

A successful blog that features over 25 interviews and has over 900 followers at time of writing. The interviews cover an international industry with writers from some of the biggest companies in UX and tech, like Dropbox, Headspace and Deliveroo.
